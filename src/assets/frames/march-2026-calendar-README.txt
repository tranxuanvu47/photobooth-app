March 2026 Calendar Frame Overlay
==================================

This file should be replaced with: march-2026-calendar.png

Image Requirements:
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Dimensions: 1200x1800 pixels (matches 4x6 portrait layout at 300 DPI)
- Content: March 2026 calendar overlay
  - Top portion: Transparent area for photo (square, approximately 1000x1000)
  - Bottom portion: March 2026 calendar grid
  - The 8th day should be marked with a heart symbol
- Transparency: The photo area should be fully transparent
- Background: Calendar area can have background color/texture

The overlay will be positioned to cover the entire canvas, with the photo slot
showing through the transparent area at the top.
